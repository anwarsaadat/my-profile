I am an M. Phil Scholar at Quaid-i-Azam University Islamabad, working with Assistant Professor Dr. Akmal Saeed Khattak. I have completed my bachelor's degree in 2021 from the University of Buner under the supervision of Dr. Inayat Khan.

My final year research focuses on Point-of-Interest Recommendation using Graph Neural Network. The purpose of the research is to bridge the gap in personalized recommendation, incorporating spatial, temporal, social, and textual features using a graph neural network. My objective is to solve complex social problems by harnessing the power of graph neural networks to improve people's lives and provide them with relevant information.

In addition to my research background, I have hands-on experience in machine learning, deep learning, graph neural networks, and data science. Furthermore, I possess skills related to web technologies, databases, designing workflows & architectures, and project management skills, which I acquired through Google's courses on Coursera.
